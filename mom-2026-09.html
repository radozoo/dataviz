Trump Has Lost Support Across Every Group

Approval ratings fell in all 13 demographic groups between February 2025 and February 2026. Latino Americans and those aged 35–49 saw the steepest drops, each falling 19 percentage points.

Viz

This visual is my submission for MakeoverMonday 2026 Week 9 — a weekly data visualization challenge where participants reimagine an existing chart using the same dataset.

The data

The data covers Trump's approval ratings across 13 demographic groups, comparing February 2025 and February 2026. Every single group moved in the same direction.

The process

I handed the dataset to Claude Code with a simple brief — make something interesting. What followed was a back-and-forth: CC proposed the structure, I reacted, we iterated. The dot plot with delta bars emerged through that conversation, not from a predefined template.

This is the workflow I'm trying to figure out — using AI to close the gap between having an idea and actually executing it.
